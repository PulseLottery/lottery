The SimpleLottery contract is a basic lottery system where participants can deposit funds and have a chance to win prizes in random drawings. Here are the rules of this lottery:

  1. Participation: - To participate in the lottery, a participant needs to call the `deposit()` function and deposit at least 2000 PLS. - If the participant has not participated before, they will be added to the list of participants. - Upon deposit, the participant's funds are distributed into four "vaults": the hourly vault, daily vault, weekly vault, and monthly vault.
  2. Rewards: Rewards are awarded in random drawings. The contract has four types of rewards: hourly, daily, weekly, and monthly. The rewards are paid out from the corresponding vault that accumulates the deposited funds.
  3. Rewards: - Rewards are awarded in random drawings from each vault. - The contract has four types of rewards: hourly, daily, weekly, and monthly. - Each vault accumulates deposited funds, and it is cleared when a reward is distributed. - The percentage distribution in each vault is defined by the variables `hourlyVaultPercentage`, `dailyVaultPercentage`, `weeklyVaultPercentage`, and `monthlyVaultPercentage`.
  4. Reward Distribution: - To claim a reward, a participant needs to call the corresponding function based on the type of reward they want to claim. For example, the `sendHourlyReward()` function distributes the hourly reward. - There are specific time constraints that need to be met before a reward can be claimed. For example, for the hourly reward, at least 1 hour must have passed since the last hourly reward distribution. - The winner is randomly selected from the list of participants based on a random index generated using factors like time and participant's address. - The winner receives a reward, which consists of a portion of the funds from the corresponding vault, and a commission (2% of the funds) is deducted and sent to a specific address.
  5. Clearing Participants List: - After the weekly and monthly reward distributions, the participants list is cleared by removing all participant addresses. - To participate in the next drawing, participants will need to deposit funds again.

These are the basic principles of operation and participation described in the `SimpleLottery` contract.
